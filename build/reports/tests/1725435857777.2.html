08-Sep-2023
The Pet Bet with Dr. Krishanu Ghosh - Part 2 | Rains & Risks
In this informative video, we dive into the fascinating topic of monsoon and its impact on our beloved pets. Join us as we sit down with renowned veterinarian, Dr. Krishanu Ghosh, to explore the various challenges and precautions pet parents should take during the monsoon season. The monsoon brings its own set of unique considerations for pet care, and it's crucial to understand how it can affect our furry companions. Dr. Ghosh shares valuable insights, practical tips, and expert advice on how to ensure the well-being and safety of our pets during this rainy season. From potential health risks, such as fungal infections and vector-borne diseases, to safety precautions for walks and outdoor activities, this video covers a wide range of important topics. Learn about proper grooming routines, managing hygiene, dealing with common monsoon ailments, and creating a comfortable indoor environment for your pets. Join us as we navigate through the challenges and opportunities presented by the monsoon season. Let's empower ourselves with knowledge to provide the best possible care for our furry friends and keep them happy and healthy all year round. Don't miss this insightful discussion with Dr. Krishanu Ghosh, a trusted authority in veterinary care. Remember to like, share, and subscribe to our channel for more informative content on pet care, health, and well-being.
